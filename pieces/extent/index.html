Part II / Improvisations with live electronics: a study in the science of performance

Trijeet Mukhopadhyay and Luigi Sambuy

Music is mainly considered an Art, but there is much science under the devices that musicians use everyday to write electronic music. Through a journey of improvised compositions and artful sound design, the duo present a live performance focused on the pillars of sound synthesis. Expect granular soundscapes, sample mangling and earth shaking basslines. Get your feet ready for dancing, but, most importantly, your mind ready to explore new sonic structures.
